# Naukri Resume Headline

The resume headline on Naukri is a short one-liner shown next to your name in recruiter search results. It's the first text after your name — treat it like a Twitter bio for your career.

---

## Current (old)

```
AI Engineer | Software Engineering | Interested in evolving memory for LLMs and context distillation
```

Not bad — it covers both AI and SWE, and the last part is genuinely interesting. But "interested in" is passive. It sounds like a goal, not a qualification. Recruiters are looking for what you can do now, not what you're learning.

---

## Updated headline (paste this)

```
AI Engineer | RAG Systems & LLM Applications | IEEE Published | Open to AI & SWE Roles
```

Why this works:
- "AI Engineer" matches the role title recruiters search for
- "RAG Systems & LLM Applications" — two terms that are highly searched right now
- "IEEE Published" — a hard differentiator; most applicants at this stage don't have publications
- "Open to AI & SWE Roles" — signals availability and flexibility

---

## Alternative — if targeting research-adjacent teams

```
AI Engineer | Graph RAG · LangGraph · Production LLMs | IEEE Published · Patent Filed
```

This leads with specific technical terms and adds the patent, which is useful when applying to teams doing serious AI infrastructure work.

---

## Note on Naukri character limits

Naukri's headline field accepts around 250 characters. All options above are well within that.
