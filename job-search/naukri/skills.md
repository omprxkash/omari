# Naukri Skills

Naukri's skills section has a hard limit and uses a predefined list. The order matters — skills listed first are weighted higher in recruiter keyword search. The current profile has IoT, Blockchain, and DBMS mixed in at the top, which pulls the focus away from the AI/SWE positioning.

---

## Current problems

- IoT, Blockchain, SaaS are listed but not relevant to the target roles
- OOPS appears as a duplicate of OOP
- Generic terms like "Cloud" and "Auth" are not Naukri-recognised skill terms — they need to be specific (AWS, OAuth)
- The order doesn't reflect priority — Python and RAG should be at the top, not in the middle

## Note on Naukri's skill validation

Naukri has a "Test your skills" feature that lets you take quick assessments and show a verified badge. If you take the Python and SQL assessments and score well, the verified badge appears next to those skills in recruiter search results — worth spending 15 minutes on each.
