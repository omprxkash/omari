## What changed and why

**Original:** *"Supported large-scale PLM and enterprise management platforms for global manufacturing clients."*
**Rewritten:** First bullet leads with the technique (Graph RAG) and the result (23%).

The original bullet was accurate but reads like a job description. The rewritten version reads like a result someone achieved.

**Rule used consistently across all bullets:** Action verb → what was built/done → method → measurable outcome. If there's no number, the scale or consequence needs to be clear from the context.
