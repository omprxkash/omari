# Certifications

Certifications are useful for two things: structured learning paths and proof for resumes/profiles. The ones below are from reputable providers and are either free or auditable.

A cert only matters if you actually learned something doing it. A cert with no projects is just paperwork.

---

## AI & ML

**DeepLearning.AI Courses**
https://www.deeplearning.ai/courses/
Andrew Ng's platform. Covers ML specialization, LLMs, MLOps, LangChain, and more. Many are free to audit.

---

## Cloud

**AWS Cloud Practitioner Essentials** (AWS SkillBuilder)
https://skillbuilder.aws/learn/94T2BEN85A/aws-cloud-practitioner-essentials/8D79F3AVR7
Official AWS course. Free. Foundation for the CLF-C02 certification.

---

## General Technical

**Google Skills**
https://www.skills.google/catalog
Google's certification catalog — covers data analytics, IT support, cybersecurity, and project management.

**IBM SkillsBuild**
https://skillsbuild.org/
Free courses and certifications from IBM across AI, cloud, data, and cybersecurity.

---

## Data Engineering

**Data Engineering Zoomcamp** (DataTalksClub)
https://github.com/DataTalksClub/data-engineering-zoomcamp
Free 9-week course covering data pipelines, data warehouses, dbt, Spark, Kafka. Cohort-based but materials are always available.
